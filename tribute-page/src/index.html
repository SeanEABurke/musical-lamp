Leonardo da Vinci

Renaissance Man

Here is a timeline of da Vinci's life:

  • 1452 - Born on April 15, in Vinci
  • 1467 - He was sent off to art school
  • 1472 - Joined the Painter's Guild of Florence
  • 1482 - Moved to Milan
  • 1490's - Began writing his famous notebooks
  • 1495 - Painted "The Last Supper"
  • 1500 - Returned to Florence
  • 1503 - Painted "The Mona Lisa"
  • 1506 - Returned to Milan to work as an architect
  • 1519 - Leonardo died in France
